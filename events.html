Cafe Events

We believe that the best events come from within our community. This is why we support local artists and social organizations with our space. Every month the cafe hosts a diverse collection of events that allow our neighborhood hosts to share their passion and talent with the community. We facilitate as many events as possible that not only enrich our neighborhood, but are also free of charge and open to all residents.Interested in hosting an event? Click here and fill out our event form, and email it to us at kusanyacafe@gmail.com.
